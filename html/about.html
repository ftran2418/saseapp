ABOUT SASE AT VT HISTORY OF SASE How to sign up

We host companies such as Capital One and Excella to educate our members on how to get hired and what options their company may have for you. We also hold professional workshops to improve your resumes, prepare you for interviews, and much more. On top of all that, we have monthly general body meetings for our members to know each better and have fun (they also have food)! To find out more on our events, go to our [events] page.

Another big part of our organization is our mentor/mentee program where you can get paired with a mentor to help you adjust to Virginia Tech if you are a new student, help you with your courses, or just be a guide to you in life! We try our best to match our pairs according to personality and majors.

SASE was started in 2007 in response to other professional organizations that were geared towards specific minority groups.
